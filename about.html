About Website: "90's games" is a website about games from decades ago that had a massive impact on the gaming industry as a whole. Three games from the 1990s,: Fallout, Half-Life, and Grim Fandango, revolutionized their own genres. The genres are: first-person shooters (FPS), role-playing, and adventure. On the website, you will learn about the mentioned games, their history, and their influence. I hope you will enjoy!
